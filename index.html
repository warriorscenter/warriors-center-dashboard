Unlike Occupancy above, Rank Breakdown counts everyone who appears anywhere in a center's census — including Grad House and Staff House — not just people in center beds. Occupancy is sourced from each center's ground-truth bed census (room-by-room, updated near-daily), not from the summary metrics workbook. It counts anyone physically sleeping in a center bed — numbered rooms plus any cot/chapel overflow — against that center's fixed center-only capacity, regardless of whether the occupant is a resident, staff member, or graduate. Grad House and Staff House beds are excluded entirely, even for a genuine program resident temporarily overflowing into one of them, because those aren't center beds. Occupancy over 100% is possible (cot/chapel overflow) and shown as-is, not capped. Staff/Grad Beds shows how many of the occupied center beds (from the figure above) are held by staff or graduates rather than active program residents (Bootcamp/Recruit/Recharge/E-1–E-9) — this can be a large share at some centers and is worth watching alongside the raw occupancy number. Rank Breakdown shows the full program population split out by exact rank, from the same daily bed census source, including Grad House/Staff House residents that Occupancy excludes. Known gap: at centers where real residents overflow into Staff House, those beds aren't counted anywhere in the Occupancy or Staff/Grad Beds figures above (Staff House sits outside the tracked center capacity entirely), though they are still counted in Rank Breakdown. Center names in the tables above link to that day's full bed census. Avg LOS restricted to residents with Entry Date on or after 1/1/25 (when client-level tracking began). Bootcamp completion = non-recharge residents with Entry Date this year, excluding the most recent 30 days as a maturity buffer. Recharge % is a separate metric from the summary metrics workbook (current Recharge count ÷ current core-program count), not derived from the bed census. Financial metrics (cost/bed, net margin) are intentionally excluded here since they only refresh monthly with the P&L close — see the monthly Director Review deck for those. Data refreshes daily. Trend arrows compare to 7 days ago (occupancy, net flow, recharge) or 30 days ago (bootcamp rate, LOS, graduates) once enough history has accumulated.
